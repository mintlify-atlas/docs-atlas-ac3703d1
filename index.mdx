## What Mi Cole Does

Mi Cole connects all participants in a school's daily operations through a single, unified app. The platform is multi-tenant by design — a super-admin creates and configures schools, each with its own director, staff, and student body.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Clone the repository">
    Clone the Flutter project and install dependencies with `flutter pub get`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the API base URL">
    Update the `baseUrl` in `lib/core/dio/dio_client.dart` to point to your backend server.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run the app">
    Launch on your target platform with `flutter run`. The app opens on the login screen and redirects based on your role after authentication.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore the docs">
    Follow the [Quickstart](/quickstart) guide for a complete walkthrough, or jump straight to the [Features](/features/tenant-management) section for a tour of each module.
  </Step>
</Steps>
